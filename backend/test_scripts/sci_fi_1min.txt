EXT. NEON STREET - NIGHT

Rain slogs down, mixed with the neon sludge of Sector 7.

KAI (20s, cybernetic eye glowing red) sprints through the downpour. Behind him, a HOVER-DRONE buzzes, its searchlight slicing the dark.

KAI
(into comms)
I'm at the junction. Where's the extract?

VOICE (V.O.)
Thirty seconds, Kai. Hold them off.

Kai skids to a halt. A dead end. He turns, drawing a pulse-blade. The DRONE hovers, its gatling guns spinning up.

KAI
Let's dance.

He lunges. The drone fires. Tracers shred the brickwork. Kai slides under the fire, leaps, and drives the blade into the drone's sensor-eye.

EXPLOSION.

Kai rolls through the smoke as a HOVER-VAN screeches to a halt. The side door slides open.

VOICE (O.S.)
Get in!

Kai dives. The van roars away, leaving the wreckage behind.

FADE OUT.
